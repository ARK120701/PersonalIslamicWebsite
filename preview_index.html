Personal History

Biography

A life devoted to the pursuit of knowledge and service to the Ummah

Abdurrahman Khan was raised in Flushing, New York, where he received his early education at Flushing Muslim Center from kindergarten through third grade. He subsequently enrolled at Darul Uloom New York, where he completed his Nāẓirah and then dedicated himself to the memorisation of the Holy Qurʾān. He formally completed his Ḥifẓ in 2014 after approximately two and a half years of memorisation, followed by an additional year and a half of intensive revision (Daur), concluding his time at Darul Uloom New York in 2015.

He thereafter attended Al-Mamoor School for the eighth and ninth grades, graduating as Valedictorian of his eighth-grade class. He subsequently completed grades ten through twelve at New Hyde Park Memorial High School in two years, pursuing advanced-level coursework and graduating with a weighted GPA of 95 and academic honours.

Following high school, Abdurrahman Khan enrolled in the ʿĀlimiyyah programme at Jamia Qurania Academy, simultaneously pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at CUNY Queens College. After three years, he transferred to Darul Uloom New York to complete the final three years of the ʿĀlimiyyah programme. He graduated from Darul Uloom New York in August 2025 and was concurrently awarded a Master's degree in Islamic Sciences from Jamia Binoria. His undergraduate studies were completed in January 2026, graduating Cum Laude with a cumulative GPA of 3.67.

Following his graduation, Abdurrahman Khan commenced the Takhaṣṣuṣ programme in Islamic Jurisprudence and the Issuance of Legal Verdicts (Iftāʾ) under Mufti Manzoor and Mufti Mizaanur Rahman. He remains committed to the integration of traditional Islamic learning and modern intellectual inquiry.
